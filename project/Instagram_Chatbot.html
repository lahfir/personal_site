Day Trader Generated $12,000+ in a Single Month from his Instagram

Chat Marketing, ChatBot Development

Service

ManyChat, Zapier Integration

Client

Madcharts - United States

Year

2023

Intro

The client is a day trader who provides signals to his premium telegram channel. The client had a telegram bot that provided long information to the users about the signals group, but he was struggling to collect payments and increase engagement with his subscribers.

The client approached us to help him optimize his subscription process, increase his email lists and improve customer engagement.

Challenges

⦾ The previous subscription procedure was difficult and time-consuming.

⦾ When users wanted to subscribe to the premium channel, they had to go through a difficult process.

⦾ High abandonment rates as a result of the challenging subscription process.

⦾ The customer was not gathering data from the client.

⦾ A lack of consumer information makes it difficult to upsell and cross-sell services.

Solution

🔥 We suggested integrating ManyChat as a remedy for the client's problems.

🔥 ManyChat offers an interactive conversation flow that gathers client data, enhances client satisfaction, and boosts engagement.

🔥 The client's Telegram and Instagram were merged with ManyChat, and we built an interactive conversation flow that made the subscription process simpler.

integration of the Stripe Payment gateway to reduce service fees when collecting payments.

🔥 Added beautiful graphics and galleries to make the discourse more engaging

🔥 The new subscription procedure was easier to use and more streamlined.

🔥 There were fewer drop-offs and higher conversions as a result of the shortened process.

🔥 The new subscription procedure made it easier to gather consumer data, which enhanced the client's capacity to cross-sell and upsell his services.

🔥 The integration of the interactive conversation flow provided by ManyChat enhanced the overall user experience.

Results

Increased Revenue

✅The first month's revenue of $6,000+ represented a 25% increase over the prior month's income.



Increased Subscriber Base

✅ The premium channel's subscriber base increased by 35%.

✅ Fewer drop-offs and greater conversions were the results of the simplified subscription procedure.

Huge Increase in Email Lists

✅ ManyChat's interactive chat flow assisted in the information-gathering process, which led to a significant increase in email lists.

✅ Now the customer could cross-sell and upsell his products to his followers.

Increased Lifetime Purchases

✅ A 40% increase in lifetime purchases was observed as a result of the chat flow's addition of eye-catching graphics and galleries.

✅ Also, consumer retention improved with the revised subscription procedure.

Increased Social Media Engagement

✅ The interactive chat flows of ManyChat encouraged users to share their experiences with others, which increased social media engagement.

✅ New subscribers and prospective customers were attracted as a result of the increased social media interaction.

Conclusion

The integration with ManyChat was successful. The findings demonstrated that ManyChat's dynamic conversation flow enhanced customer satisfaction, increased engagement, and increased sales. The client was able to enhance social media engagement, streamline the subscription process, gather customer information, and upsell and cross-sell services using ManyChat. The capabilities of ManyChat turned out to be game-changers for the client's business, and we advise any company wishing to enhance customer experience and spur growth to use it.
