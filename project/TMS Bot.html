Trust My Stocks is a company that provides a signal sharing service on Telegram for a monthly subscription fee. The client approached me with the need to automate the process of acquiring new clients and managing them on an interactive dashboard. I was tasked with developing a Telegram bot with best coding practices and creating a CRM dashboard using Flask, HTML, CSS, and JS. The backend stack of the application was kept in Python, allowing the client to communicate with their members through the dashboard. Additionally, I integrated Stripe and Paypal to collect payments.

Process

To achieve the project goals, I employed a four-step process.

Firstly, I conducted thorough research on the signal sharing service industry and identified the best coding practices for creating a Telegram bot and a CRM dashboard.

Secondly, I created a prototype of the system and conducted multiple rounds of testing and modification to ensure that it was bug-free and easy to use.

Thirdly, I integrated Stripe and Paypal to collect payments, ensuring that the payment process was secure and reliable.

Finally, I deployed the system and conducted performance checks to ensure that it was running smoothly.

Results

The deployment of the automated system was a huge success. Within the first two hours of deployment, the Telegram bot had over 600 real-time conversations with potential clients. Out of those, 323 people signed up for a monthly subscription, resulting in a 53% conversion rate. The CRM dashboard was user-friendly and provided an intuitive user experience, which contributed to the high conversion rate. The integration of Stripe and Paypal was successful, and the payment process was secure and reliable.
