SFT Social Trading App

Cross platform mobile app - Front End

Service

Front End - React Native

Client

Swiss Forex Trading SA - Switzerland

Year

2021

Intro

The client, a Switzerland-based startup, wanted to create a trading app with social media features for forex traders. They had a tight deadline and were seeking to hire a frontend mobile developer to assist with the project. I was approached by the client to aid in the development process.

Project Goals

⦾ The main objective of the project was to create a trading app that incorporated social media features for forex traders.

⦾ The client wanted the app to be user-friendly, bug-free, and able to perform at its peak capacity.

⦾ In addition, the client wanted the app to attract a large number of users within a short period of time.

Challenges

The client had a limited amount of time to launch the app and needed to hire a frontend mobile developer to assist with the project. I was brought on board to fill that role. Additionally, the client required an app that was user-friendly and bug-free, with top-notch performance.

Process

To achieve the goals set out by the client, I employed a three-step process. Firstly, I conducted thorough research on forex trading and social media apps to ensure that our product was up to industry standards. Secondly, I created a prototype of the app, which underwent multiple rounds of testing and modification before being finalized. Finally, I launched the app, conducting bug testing and performance checks.

Results

The launch of the app was a massive success. Within the first hour of launching, over 1,000 users registered on the app. With the current marketing campaign in effect, the app is expected to attract even more users. The app performed optimally, with minimal bugs and a consistent 60fps throughout. The user interface was intuitive, providing a user-friendly experience.

Impact

The app has made it easier for forex traders to connect with each other and share their experiences. By providing social media functionality, the app has helped create a sense of community within the forex trading world. Additionally, the app has created jobs for frontend mobile developers in the local community, helping to boost the local economy.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the development of the trading app with social media features for forex traders was a resounding success. The app has been well-received by users, with a significant number of registrations within the first hour of launch. The app was created with a user-centric approach and has performed optimally, with minimal bugs and consistent 60fps. The development of the app has had a positive impact on the local community by creating jobs for frontend mobile developers and helping to create a sense of community within the forex trading world.
